Introducing Redmi Note 14 Pro 5G, a sophisticated mid-range smartphone built to deliver premium performance, stunning visuals, and versatile photography all in one sleek package. Designed for users who demand balance—powerful hardware meets refined style, making it ideal for daily productivity, entertainment, and creative expression.

At the heart of this device is the MediaTek Dimensity 7300 Ultra chipset, engineered for smooth multitasking and responsive performance whether you’re gaming, streaming, or switching between apps. The expansive 6.67-inch 1.5K AMOLED display with a 120 Hz refresh rate delivers vibrant colors, deep blacks, and ultra-fluid visuals that bring every frame to life, day or night.

Capture life in rich detail with the 50 MP Sony LYT600 primary camera, supported by an 8 MP ultra-wide lens and 2 MP macro lens—perfect for landscapes, group photos, and close-ups alike. The front features a 20 MP selfie camera that ensures sharp and expressive portraits and video calls.

Stay powered through long days with a 5500 mAh battery and 45 W fast charging, so you’re ready for anything without frequent stops at the charger. Built tough yet stylish, this smartphone offers IP68 water and dust resistance and Corning® Gorilla® Glass Victus 2 protection for durability you can count on.

Running on the intuitive Android-based HyperOS, the Redmi Note 14 Pro combines power and sophistication with practical everyday features that elevate your mobile experience.
