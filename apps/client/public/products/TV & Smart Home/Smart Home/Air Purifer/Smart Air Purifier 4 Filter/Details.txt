The Xiaomi Smart Air Purifier 4 Filter is a replacement filter element for the Xiaomi Smart Air Purifier 4. It is designed to help the air purifier maintain clean and healthy indoor air by capturing dust, pollen, smoke, pet dander, and other fine airborne particles through its multi-layer filtration system. Regular replacement keeps the purifier working efficiently and ensures better air quality for your home.

✨ Key Features

Three-in-one Filtration System: Combines a primary pre-filter, high-efficiency filter media, and activated carbon to trap particles and odors from the air.

High Filtration Efficiency: Removes fine particles like PM2.5, pollen, and smoke effectively, helping keep indoor air cleaner.

Activated Carbon Layer: Helps absorb odours such as pet smells, cooking fumes, and volatile organic compounds (VOCs).

Easy Replacement: Designed for quick and hassle-free filter changes in the Smart Air Purifier 4.

Recommended Lifespan: Replace approximately every 6–12 months depending on usage and air quality.
