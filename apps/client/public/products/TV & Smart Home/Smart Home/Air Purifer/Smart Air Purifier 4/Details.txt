Xiaomi Smart Air Purifier 4

The Xiaomi Smart Air Purifier 4 is a smart air purification device designed to clean indoor air effectively by removing dust, pollen, smoke, pet dander, and other airborne particles. It uses advanced triple‑layer filtration and a high‑efficiency filter to help you breathe fresher, healthier air at home or in the office.

🌬️ Key Features (Easy to Understand)

✔ Powerful Filtration:

High‑Efficiency Filter (primary + HEPA + activated carbon) removes up to 99.97% of particles as small as 0.3 μm.

Activated carbon layer helps reduce odors and harmful gases like VOCs.

✔ Fast Air Cleaning:

360° all‑round air intake and a Particle CADR up to 400 m³/h can purify air in a medium room quickly.

Can clean the air in about 10 minutes in a 20 m² room.

✔ Smart & Convenient:

Real‑time air quality monitoring via built‑in sensors and an OLED display shows PM2.5 levels.

Smart app control through the Mi Home/Xiaomi Home app lets you operate it remotely.

Voice control support with Google Assistant and Alexa allows hands‑free operation.

✔ Quiet & Comfortable:

Low‑noise operation (~32.1 dB in Night mode) makes it suitable for bedrooms.

✔ Long‑Lasting Filter:

Filter lasts about 6–12 months before replacement is needed.
