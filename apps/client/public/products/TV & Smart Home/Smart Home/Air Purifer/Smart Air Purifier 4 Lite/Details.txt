360° Air Intake & Fast Cleaning: Powerful 360 m³/h CADR delivers clean air quickly throughout the room.

Wide Coverage: Effective for rooms around 269 – 463 sq ft, ideal for bedrooms, living rooms, and offices.

Smart Controls: Supports the Mi Home / Xiaomi Home app for remote control, scheduling, and monitoring air quality.
