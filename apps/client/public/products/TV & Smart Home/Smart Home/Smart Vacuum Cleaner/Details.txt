Xiaomi Robot Vacuum Cleaner S10

The Xiaomi Robot Vacuum Cleaner S10 is a smart robot vacuum and mop combo designed to automate floor cleaning in your home. It uses advanced laser navigation and mapping to move efficiently around rooms, removing dust, dirt and debris with strong suction — and it also offers mopping for deeper cleaning.

✨ Key Features

4000 Pa Turbo Suction: Powerful suction removes dust, hair, and debris from floors and carpets.

LDS Laser Navigation & Mapping: Uses a laser distance sensor to precisely map your home and plan smart cleaning routes.

2-in-1 Vacuum + Mop: Integrated water tank and dust container let it vacuum and mop simultaneously for cleaner floors.

Zone Cleaning & Virtual Walls: Set specific cleaning zones or no-go areas from the app for customized cleaning.

Long Battery Life: 3200 mAh battery provides up to around 140 minutes of runtime on a full charge.

App Control: Connect to the Mi Home / Xiaomi Home app to schedule, monitor and control cleaning remotely.
