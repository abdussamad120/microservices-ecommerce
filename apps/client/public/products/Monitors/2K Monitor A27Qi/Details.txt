The Xiaomi 2K Monitor A27Qi is a 27-inch 2K (QHD) display designed for both productivity and entertainment, offering sharp visuals, accurate colours, and smooth performance — perfect for work, streaming, and everyday use.

✨ Key Features

📊 2K QHD Clarity
• 2560 × 1440 resolution delivers detailed text, crisp images, and clearer visuals than Full HD.

🌈 Accurate Colours & Wide Gamut
• Covers 95% DCI-P3 and 100% sRGB, showing rich and natural colours — great for photos, videos, and creative work.
• 16.7 million colours with factory colour calibration for reliable colour reproduction.

⚡ Smooth Performance
• 100 Hz refresh rate (some versions support up to ~120 Hz on compatible devices) for smoother motion and scrolling.
• 6 ms response time for fluid visuals with minimal blur on everyday tasks.

👁️ Eye Comfort & Wide View
• 178° wide viewing angle IPS panel ensures consistent colours from different angles.
• Low Blue Light & DC Dimming (TÜV certification) helps reduce eye strain during long sessions.

🔌 Connectivity & Design
• Includes DisplayPort 1.4, HDMI 2.0, and audio out ports for versatile connections.
• Slim bezels and VESA mount support make it ideal for multi-monitor setups or wall mounting.
