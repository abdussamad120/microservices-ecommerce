The Xiaomi Bluetooth Voice Remote is a smart, ergonomic remote control designed to elevate your TV entertainment experience by combining Bluetooth connectivity with voice-enabled control via Google Assistant. It’s built primarily for Mi, Redmi, and Xiaomi Smart TVs running Android/Google TV platforms, and also works with compatible TV sticks and set-top devices.

📺 Key Features

🔊 Wireless Bluetooth Connectivity
Connects seamlessly to your smart TV or streaming device without needing to point directly at an IR sensor — offering more freedom and reliability than traditional remotes.

🎤 Voice Assistant Integration
Built-in microphone lets you activate Google Assistant by pressing the voice button and speaking commands — search content, launch apps, control playback, and more using voice.

🎥 Dedicated Hotkeys
Convenient shortcut buttons for popular streaming services like Netflix, Prime Video, and YouTube make it fast to jump straight into your favorite apps.

🖐️ Comfortable Ergonomic Design
Lightweight and compact with a smooth button layout — ideal for everyday use and long binge-watch sessions.

📦 Universal Compatibility
Works with many Xiaomi/Redmi Smart TVs and Android/Google TV devices (including TV sticks and boxes) that support Bluetooth remotes.
