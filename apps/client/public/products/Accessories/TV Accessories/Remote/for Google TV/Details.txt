The Mi Bluetooth Voice Remote is a user-friendly Bluetooth remote control designed to enhance your smart TV experience, especially on Google TV / Android TV platforms. It replaces traditional IR remotes with a wireless Bluetooth connection, offering voice-enabled control through Google Assistant for effortless navigation, content search, and smart commands.

📺 Key Features

Bluetooth Connectivity: Connects wirelessly without needing line-of-sight — you can control your TV or streaming device from anywhere in the room.

Voice Control with Google Assistant: Built-in microphone lets you press the mic button and speak commands to search content, open apps, adjust settings, and more.

Dedicated Hotkeys: Quick access buttons for popular streaming apps like Netflix, Prime Video and sometimes YouTube/Hotstar make entertainment one-tap easy.

Ergonomic Design: Slim, lightweight remote that’s comfortable to hold and use for long viewing sessions.

Universal Compatibility: Works with Xiaomi/Redmi Smart TVs, TV Sticks, and many Android/Google TV systems — check exact model support before pairing.

No Line-of-Sight Needed: Bluetooth connection means you don’t have to aim directly at the TV sensor.
