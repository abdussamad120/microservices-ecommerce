📌 Simple Product Description
The Xiaomi Mi TV Stick is a small streaming device that turns any TV with an HDMI port into a smart TV. Once plugged in, it runs Android TV, giving you access to popular streaming apps like Netflix, YouTube, Prime Video, Disney+ Hotstar, and more. It supports Full HD (1080p) streaming and lets you control content with a Bluetooth remote that includes Google Assistant voice search.

✨ Key Points

Converts regular TV into Smart TV via HDMI.

Runs Android TV with access to apps and games.

Built-in Chromecast lets you cast from your phone or tablet.

Bluetooth remote with voice control (Google Assistant).

Supports Wi-Fi & Bluetooth connectivity
