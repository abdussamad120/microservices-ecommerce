Mi Box 4K
₹3,999

The Xiaomi Mi Box 4K is a compact Android TV streaming device that turns any TV with an HDMI port into a Smart TV. It lets you stream movies, shows, and videos in 4K Ultra-HD with HDR10 quality from popular apps like Netflix, YouTube, Prime Video and more.

It runs Android TV 9.0, gives access to Google Play Store apps, and includes built-in Chromecast for casting content from your phone or tablet. You also get a voice remote with Google Assistant for easy control.

✨ Key Points

Supports 4K UHD streaming at up to 60 fps with HDR10.

Runs Android TV, offering apps and games from the Play Store.

Chromecast built-in lets you cast from mobiles/tablets.

Includes Bluetooth voice remote (Google Assistant).

Connects via Wi-Fi & Bluetooth, with HDMI and USB ports.
