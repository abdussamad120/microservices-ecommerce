Xiaomi 15 Ultra Photography Kit

₹11,999

The Xiaomi 15 Ultra Photography Kit is an official accessory pack designed to enhance the photography experience of the Xiaomi 15 Ultra smartphone. It adds DSLR-style controls and tools to your phone, letting you shoot photos and videos more comfortably and creatively.

📸 Key Features (Simple)

Camera Grip with Controls: A comfortable grip that connects via USB-C and offers physical buttons like a shutter button and dials for exposure, ISO, white balance, and zoom.

Detachable Shutter Button: Works like a real camera — half-press to focus and full-press to shoot.

Extra Battery: The grip includes a built-in 2,000 mAh battery to give extra power during long shooting sessions.

Filter Adapter (67 mm): Lets you attach professional filters (like ND or polarizers) for better creative shots (filters sold separately).

Protective Case: The kit comes with a case that protects the phone and works with the grip.

Ergonomic Design: Thumb rest and grip make handling easier and more stable while shooting.
