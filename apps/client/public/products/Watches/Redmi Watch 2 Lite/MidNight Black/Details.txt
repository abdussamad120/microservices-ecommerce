The Redmi Watch 2 Lite is an affordable smartwatch from Xiaomi’s Redmi lineup with a big 1.55″ touch display, built-in GPS, and comprehensive health & fitness tracking features — perfect for everyday use, workouts, and staying connected.

✨ Key Features

📺 1.55″ Colour Touch Display – Clear and bright screen for easy viewing and interaction.

🏃 100+ Fitness Modes – Tracks workouts, running, cycling, yoga, and more.

📍 Built-in GPS – Supports GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BDS for accurate outdoor tracking without a phone.

❤️ Health Monitoring – 24-hour heart rate tracking, blood oxygen (SpO₂) measurement, sleep and stress monitoring.

💧 5 ATM Water Resistance – Suitable for swimming and splashes.

🔋 Up to 10 Days Battery Life – Long battery life on a single charge under typical use.

📱 Notifications & Controls – View alerts for calls, messages, music control, and more via the Xiaomi Wear/Mi Fitness app.
