The Redmi Watch 5 Active is a feature-packed smartwatch from Xiaomi’s Redmi lineup that combines fitness tracking, health monitoring, smart features, and Bluetooth calling in a sleek, modern design. It’s powered by HyperOS, has a large 2″ display, long battery life, and supports voice assistant Alexa, making it ideal for everyday use and active lifestyles.

✨ Key Features

Large 2″ LCD Display: Bright screen (up to 500 nits) with clear visuals and easy navigation.

Bluetooth Calling: Make and receive calls directly from the watch with built-in speaker and mic (3-mic ENC).

Long Battery Life: Up to ~18 days on a single charge with typical use (about 12 days on heavy use).

Health Monitoring: 24/7 heart rate, SpO₂ (blood oxygen) tracking, stress and sleep monitoring.

140+ Sports Modes: Tracks a variety of workouts and activities.

Alexa Voice Assistant: Built-in voice assistant support for hands-free help.

IPX8 Water Resistance: Suitable for daily water exposure like rain or sweat.

Customizable Watch Faces: 200+ faces to suit your style.

Compatibility: Works with Android and iOS devices via the Mi Fitness/Xiaomi Wear app.
