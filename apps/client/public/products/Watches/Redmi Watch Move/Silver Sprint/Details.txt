Redmi Watch Move

₹1,999

The Redmi Watch Move is a budget-friendly smart wearable from Xiaomi’s Redmi lineup that offers a bright AMOLED screen, long battery life, health monitoring, fitness tracking, and Bluetooth calling — all packed into a sleek, comfortable smartwatch design.

📊 Key Features

📺 Large AMOLED Display
• 1.85-inch AMOLED screen with 390 × 450 resolution and 600 nits brightness for clear and vibrant visuals. Supports Always-On Display.

💪 Health & Fitness Tracking
• Tracks heart rate, SpO₂ (blood oxygen), sleep (including REM), stress, and supports female health monitoring.
• 140+ workout modes for diverse activities — including running, cycling, walking, rope skipping, and more.

📞 Smart & Communication Features
• Bluetooth calling with call notifications, call history, and up to 10 favorite contacts stored on the watch.
• Syncs notifications, calendar, weather, tasks, and notes via Xiaomi HyperOS.

🔋 Long Battery Life
• Up to 14 days battery life on a single charge under typical usage. Even with Always-On Display, you can get several days before recharging.

🌧️ Durable Build
• IP68 water and dust resistance for everyday wear and active lifestyles.
• Comfortable anti-allergy, skin-friendly TPU strap with quick-release mechanism.

🛠️ Easy Navigation
• Features a functional rotating crown for smooth scrolling, quick access, and intuitive control.
