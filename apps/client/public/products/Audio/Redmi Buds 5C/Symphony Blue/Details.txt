The Xiaomi Redmi Buds 5C are true-wireless Bluetooth earbuds that deliver great sound, noise reduction, and long battery life in a compact design — perfect for everyday listening, calls, and travel.

🎧 Key Features
• Hybrid Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) up to ~40 dB to reduce background noise.
• 12.4 mm titanium dynamic drivers for clear and powerful audio.
• Up to ~36 hours total playback with charging case (earbuds ~7 hours on a single charge).
• Bluetooth 5.3 with Google Fast Pair for quick and stable connections.
• IP54 splash & dust resistance for durability in light rain or workouts.
• Quick Charge: ~10 min charge gives ~2 hours playback.
• Multiple sound profiles via app (Standard, Bass, Treble, Voice, Custom).
