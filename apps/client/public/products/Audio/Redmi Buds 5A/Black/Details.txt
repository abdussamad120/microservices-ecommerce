The Redmi Buds 5A are true wireless Bluetooth earbuds from Xiaomi’s Redmi lineup that deliver clear and immersive sound, active noise cancellation, and long battery life in a lightweight, comfortable design — ideal for everyday music, calls, and travel.

🎧 Key Features

Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) – Reduces up to 25 dB of background noise for focused listening.

12 mm Dynamic Drivers – Powerful audio with rich bass and clear mids.

Bluetooth 5.4 & Google Fast Pair – Quick and reliable connection with compatible devices.

Up to ~30 Hours Playback – Long battery life with charging case (with ANC off).

Transparency Mode – Lets you hear surroundings when needed.

AI ENC for Calls – Enhanced clarity for voice calls by reducing background noise.

IPX4 Splash Resistance – Protection against sweat and light water.

Low Latency Mode (~60 ms) – Better audio sync for casual gaming.

Touch Controls – Easy music and call control with taps.
